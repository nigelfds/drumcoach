Your tempo: how fast you're actually playing, in beats per minute.
In time: how consistent your timing is overall. “Steady” means you're locked to the click.
Drift: are you gradually speeding up or slowing down? Near zero means you're holding the tempo.
Tightness: how scattered your hits are around each beat, in milliseconds. Smaller is tighter.
